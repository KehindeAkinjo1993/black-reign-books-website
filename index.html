Our Mission

Black Reign Books is a publishing liaison connecting incarcerated storytellers
with editors, designers, and readers. We provide affordable proofreading,
formatting, and cover design so every story can be told - despite systemic
barriers.

Founded by a father and daughter in Pennsylvania, we believe that the voices
behind the walls deserve to be amplified, celebrated, and preserved for
generations to come.

The Process

From your first draft to published work, we guide you every step of the way.

Barriers & Why We Exist

Censorship. Bias. Limited communication.

High costs. Restricted access. Gatekeeping.

We exist to break these walls down - one story at a time.
